Rupee Roundup

Here Is How You Play



  1. You will have a random number set to "Your Goal"
  2. Choose from four different Rupees of:
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Orange
    • Purple
  3. Clicking on a Rupee will add its current value your total score.
  4. You win the game by your total score equaling the Goal.
  5. You loose the game by your total score exceeding the Goal.
  6. The value of each Rupee is hidden, until you click it.
  7. Each time you win or loose, the Goal and value of each Rupee will change.
